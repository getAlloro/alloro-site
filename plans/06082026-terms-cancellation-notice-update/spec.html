Why

The public Terms of Service currently says clients can cancel at any time, but it does not reflect Alloro's offboarding SOP notice rules. The Terms need to preserve the no-contract promise while making final billing period and cancellation timing expectations explicit.

What

Update the Terms of Service cancellation and termination language to match the May 2026 offboarding SOP: cancellation may be requested at any time, standard cancellation is not immediate, 15 days' notice before the next billing date avoids an additional billing cycle, fewer than 15 days' notice results in one additional full cycle, and service continues through the final paid period unless a written legal exception is approved.

  • /Users/rustinedave/Downloads/Alloro_Offboarding_SOP.pdf - Source offboarding process language for cancellation, billing notice, and wind-down timing.

Patterns To Follow

  • Keep the existing Terms page structure, section numbering, Tailwind classes, and Alloro Note callout pattern.
  • Use direct, plain-English copy; do not introduce a separate legal page pattern.

Reference file: src/pages/TermsOfService.tsx - closest existing analog because this is a copy amendment inside the existing page.

Constraints

Must

  • Keep "cancel any time" and "no long-term contracts" positioning.
  • Clarify industry-standard billing notice and final paid-period rules.
  • Keep data export and ownership commitments consistent with existing Terms.

Must Not

  • Do not change operational billing code or Stripe behavior.
  • Do not update unrelated marketing copy without a proven conflict.
  • Do not create new dependencies or new page components.

Out Of Scope

  • Offboarding automation, Stripe webhook changes, account state tables, data export implementation, and website ownership policy changes outside the Terms page.

Risk

Level: 2 - Concern

Risks Identified

  • Contract-facing copy can create billing expectations that differ from operations. Mitigation: mirror only the SOP's cancellation timing rules and flag the final wording for human legal/business review.
  • The SOP contains internal-only roles and exception handling. Mitigation: do not mention Jo, Corey, internal approvals by name, N8N, or automation details in public Terms copy.

Blast radius: Public /terms route only. Consumers are src/App.tsx, src/components/marketing/MarketingFooter.tsx, src/components/Footer.tsx, src/pages/PrivacyPolicy.tsx, and src/pages/AlloroProtect.tsx as links into the route.

Pushback

  • Do not pretend "cancel any time" means "service and billing stop instantly." Future-us will hate reconciling that against Stripe and the SOP. Recommended path: "request cancellation any time" plus explicit notice/final-period terms.
